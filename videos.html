Introduction Video

Hello, my name is Dhuruv Chaudhary, and I’m currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering. I have a deep passion for web development, with a particular interest in creating interactive, intuitive, and engaging user experiences. I enjoy working on both the frontend and backend, and I’m constantly exploring new tools and technologies to enhance my development skills. Over the course of my academic journey, I’ve worked on several projects that reflect both my creativity and technical ability. Two of my major projects include: Clean Bot 3000 – a grid-based logic game where players program a cleaning robot to complete levels, combining elements of coding, problem-solving, and game design. Draw and Guess Game – a real-time multiplayer web game inspired by platforms like Skribbl.io, where players take turns drawing and guessing words, requiring smooth web socket communication and responsive UI. Through these projects, I’ve gained valuable experience in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Python, and frameworks for real-time web development. I take pride in writing clean, efficient code and enjoy working collaboratively on innovative ideas. I’m highly motivated to continue learning and building, with the goal of becoming a well-rounded developer who can contribute meaningfully to the tech industry.
